then everything else is either meh or bad

gameplay
combat system with 3 different attacks with one that can be unlocked when the character have the right attack slot
another one that buff the specificity of your character attack type and have less attack recover is only uselfull to adjust attack slot to prevent enemies to have the special attack or give your right character to have it
then you have the special attack called burst that do a good amount of damage in the specific category your character is in can be activated by having one of your character in the burst slot in the attack sequence burst attack level is tied to the rank level of your character
the synthesis system is still atelier like with some specificity added for the sake of the game it have a recycle system to trash things you craft that are not the right one to gain a specific currency to buy more usefull stuff in the shop

gacha elements
the currency for pulling characters and equipment is divided in 2 one that you earnget gifted and one you purchase which is premium a 10x pull is equal to 15k premiums crystal or 3k normal crystal
the price for premium currency to a 10x pull is 22 which less than most of gatcha but 
the banners pool system is bad since it mixes equipment and character in the end making you having less chance to pull the character of the banner
when you pull if you get a character that you already have you get a currency that can be trade for fragments of the character of your choice limited availability that can be used to rank up the character you selected
the pity system work as when you do a 10x pull you get 11 token of a currency that can be traded for a character of your choice for 150 of the said token

so in the end you are kind of forced to pay for premium currency to do a 10x pull but have the same odd to get the character of the banner than a normal banner since it has equipment in the pull too
really not good stop doing the same trash as fgo please

alteration
some cloths designs were changedtone down from previous atelier series games what is the reason we can only speculate
suelle that used guns to fight in previous atelier series games now use daggers while other characters still use their gun again what is the reason
the cigarette that keith use to do his special attack has been remove now it is just a sphere again why


my conclusion is if you really want to play it either play it casually in f2p or wait until they rework the pull system
